		<p class="lead blue">Our mission is to provide a system that will be the most reliable single source of strategic planning and performance information available to the FAA.</p>
		<p> This website will eventually replace the legacy <a href="https://spire.faa.gov/spire/login1.asp" target="_blank" data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="top" title="Visit Legacy SPIRE Site">Simplified Program Information Reporting and Evaluation (SPIRE)</a> tool as the central repository of all authenticated program information.  The migration of features and data from the legacy SPIRE tool into this application will occur over a period of time.  As they are migrated and those parts turned off in the legacy SPIRE tool, this website will be used to provide those capabilities.  After all the legacy SPIRE tool features and data are migrated, that tool will be decommissioned and only this website will remain.</p>
		<p>Further, to enhance strategic management processes, we will also be integrating the strategic and business planning capabilities into this application replacing the Business Plan Builder (BPB) tool and its (PAM) module. This will enhance program and planning reporting, as well as provide efficiencies for other Lines of Business.</p>
		<p>This new website addresses usability concerns, improves compliance with Agency requirements and refreshes the underlying technology to provide features that more closely support evolving user needs, improves navigation and presents a more intuitive user experience.</p>
		<p>Thank you for visiting the SPIRE Portal! We hope you find the redesigned site easier to use and that you will return to this site often to support your work.</p>
